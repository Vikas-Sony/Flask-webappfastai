What mushroom is that?

This webapp detects the type of mushroom from a photograph. It works using machine learning; an algorithm that takes bunch of photos of different objects and works out how to distinguish them. The model was trained using the Deep Learning techniques in fastai; it's easier than it sounds.

We distinguish the 100 most common mushroom types, thanks to wikipedia. For each type, the computer used 100 images. It get's the right mushroom in 3 out of 4 cases. If it's uncertain, it shows pictures of the ones uncertain about. This might not sound impressive, but some of the types are very similar and can only identified for sure by touching them.
